About Shyam Flour Mill

Established in 1984, Shyam Flour Mills Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in Fatehpur, Uttar Pradesh. We specialize in roller-milled premium wheat flour (chakki atta), maida, suji, and bran. Using state-of-the-art machinery while preserving traditional chakki freshness, we deliver hygienic, nutritious products to families and businesses across the region.
